About

I started this website in the summer between my sophomore and junior years of college.

I started this website because I realized, what do I have to offer? I kept thinking soon I'll be sitting in an interview where I'll get asked, "Tell us about yourself."

My gpa is good and I work a lot (three jobs), but what else can I offer about myself? What do I want people to know about me?

I realized that although my past programming classes had been difficult, I didn't have much to show for them except a passing grade and my new knowledge of a programming language. So I decided I would teach my self new skills and work on personal projects because I figured out what I wanted to showcase.

I'm invested in the technology world. I'm excited about all the new advancements in technology and all the potential yet to be achieved and I realized, I want everyone to know how dedicated and enthusiastic I am about this industry.

I have the drive and hard working skills needed to succeed. All I'm looking for now is a spot in the world.
